What is this site?
FixMyStreet is a site to help people report local issues by simply locating them on a map. It also allows people to see that the issue they have raised is being dealt with.
How do I get in touch with [% site_name %]?
Here’s our contact page.
What sort of problems should I report with [% site_name %]?
FixMyStreet in Thamesmead has been designed for reporting things which are broken or dirty or damaged or dumped, and need fixing, cleaning or clearing, such as:
  • Abandoned vehicles
  • Dog Fouling
  • Flyposting or graffiti
  • Flytipping or litter
  • Street cleaning, such as broken glass in a cycle lane
  • Unlit lamp posts
  • Potholes
What isn’t [% site_name %] for?
Please use this site only for problems like those listed above. You will need to contact our customer care line directly for problems such as:
  • Anti-social behaviour
  • Any urgent or emergency problems
  • Noise pollution or barking dogs
  • Fires and smoke/smell pollution
  • Missing wheelie bins or recycling boxes or missed rubbish collections
  • Proposals for speed bumps/ CCTV/ pedestrian crossings/ new road layouts/ etc.
  • Complaining about your neighbours
  • Complaining about the authority
  • Joy riding, drug taking, animal cruelty, or other criminal activity
How do I use the site?
After typing where you are, you will see a map of that area. You can view problems already reported in that area, or report ones of your own. Just click on the map at the location of the problem.
